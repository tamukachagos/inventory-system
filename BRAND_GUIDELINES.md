# CrimsonSupply Nexus Brand System

## Product Identity
- Product name: `CrimsonSupply Nexus`
- Tagline: `Clinical Inventory Intelligence Platform`
- Positioning: Enterprise-grade clinical inventory control with real-time analytics, predictive intelligence, and auditable workflows.

## Logo System
- Primary logo file: `frontend/public/brand-logo.svg`
- Favicon: `frontend/public/favicon.svg`
- Splash artwork: `frontend/public/splash-screen.svg`
- Mark concept:
  - Vertical bar motif references barcode scanning and traceability.
  - Plus badge indicates clinical context and supply availability.
  - Rounded geometry supports tablet-first ergonomics and modern healthcare UI tone.

## Brand Color Tokens
- `brand.crimson.700`: `#8A0F2D` (primary action color)
- `brand.crimson.500`: `#B41E3A` (active/hover accents)
- `brand.crimson.900`: `#6A0C22` (pressed/dark accents)
- `brand.navy.700`: `#1C2E4A` (secondary surfaces and data framing)
- `brand.navy.900`: `#0E1728` (dark mode base)
- `brand.rose.100`: `#F6D8DD` (soft highlight/loading accents)
- Semantic colors:
  - `success`: `#159E66`
  - `warning`: `#E68A00`
  - `danger`: `#D43D51`

## Typography Choices
- Primary family: `Aptos, Segoe UI, Trebuchet MS, Helvetica Neue, Arial, sans-serif`
- Scale:
  - `h4`: 1.6rem, 700 (headline moments)
  - `h6`: 1.05rem, 700 (section anchors)
  - `body1`: 0.95rem (operational readability)
  - `body2`: 0.85rem (secondary metadata)
- Why this stack:
  - Strong rendering on Windows tablets and iPad browsers.
  - Clean enterprise look without decorative noise.
  - High legibility in low-light warehouse conditions.

## Motion Principles
- Use motion for orientation, not decoration.
- Durations stay in 120-220ms range for interaction feedback.
- Page transitions are soft fade/slide; no abrupt cuts.
- Reduced-motion users get near-static equivalents.

## Experience Rationale
1. Trust-first enterprise feel:
   - Deep navy + crimson communicates healthcare rigor and institutional identity.
2. Operational clarity:
   - High contrast text and restrained gradients preserve dashboard readability.
3. Tablet ergonomics:
   - Rounded touch targets, clear hierarchy, and low visual clutter.
4. Brand consistency:
   - Same logo, colors, and tone applied to splash, login, shell, and system feedback.
